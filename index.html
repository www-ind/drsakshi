    💬 Ask Us Anything
    Is dental implant surgery painful? +

    No — implant surgery is performed under local anesthesia, making the procedure completely painless. Most patients report feeling only light pressure. Post-operative discomfort is minimal and manageable with standard pain relievers for 2-3 days.

    How long does a smile makeover take? +

    A smile makeover typically takes 2-4 appointments spread over 1-3 weeks, depending on the treatments involved. Simple cases like whitening + veneers can be completed in as little as one week.

    Do you offer EMI / payment options? +

    Yes! We offer 0% EMI options through major credit cards and finance partners. We also accept UPI, cash, and all major payment methods. Affordable dental care should be accessible to everyone.

    How often should I visit the dentist? +

    We recommend visiting every 6 months for routine checkups and cleaning. Regular visits help catch issues early, preventing costly treatments. Your first consultation at SKK Dental is completely free!

    Are you open on weekends? +

    We are open Monday through Saturday, 10 AM – 1 PM and 4 PM – 8 PM. We are closed on Sundays. Emergency consultations can be arranged on WhatsApp even outside working hours.

    Is the clinic hygienic and sterilised? +

    Absolutely. We maintain hospital-grade sterilisation with autoclave technology. All instruments are single-use or sterilised after each patient. We follow strict infection control protocols exceeding standard guidelines.
